    About Us

    AI-powered web application for real-time fetal health monitoring and analysis.

    Fetal health monitoring is a critical aspect of prenatal care, aiming to ensure the well-being of the fetus throughout pregnancy. Traditional monitoring methods, such as cardiotocography (CTG), often require constant supervision by medical professionals and depend on manual interpretation of complex data. This can lead to delayed diagnoses, human error, and inconsistent assessments, especially in resource-constrained settings. Additionally, communication gaps between doctors, patients, and lab technicians can further hinder effective prenatal care. There is a pressing need for an intelligent, automated, and accessible system that can analyze fetal health data accurately, generate actionable insights, and streamline communication among all stakeholders involved in the healthcare process.

    • Enhance communication between healthcare providers and patients through a centralized platform.
    • Reduce the reliance on manual interpretation and improve diagnostic accuracy and consistency.
    • Improve maternal and fetal health outcomes through timely and data-driven interventions.
